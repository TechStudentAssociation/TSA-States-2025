Our Story

At Leaf & Ladle, we believe in the transformative power of plant-based cuisine. Founded in 2023, we set out to create a space where food nourishes not only the body but the planet as well. Our mission is to offer fresh, sustainable, and incredibly flavorful dishes that make a lasting impact on your health and the environment.

Our core values are rooted in health-conscious eating, sustainability, and the joy of plant-based cuisine. We are committed to sourcing local, organic, and ethically-produced ingredients to ensure the highest quality. We aim to make vegan dining accessible and enjoyable for everyone, proving that food can indeed change the world.

Our Partner Organizations

EcoBite

EcoBite is dedicated to providing sustainable food solutions by connecting restaurants with eco-friendly suppliers. Their mission is to reduce the environmental impact of the food industry through innovative practices and partnerships.

Plant Link

Plant Link specializes in sourcing high-quality, organic plant-based ingredients. They work closely with farmers to ensure a consistent supply of fresh, nutritious produce while promoting sustainable farming practices.

Green Source

Green Source focuses on reducing food waste and promoting sustainability in the food industry. They provide resources and support to restaurants to help them implement eco-friendly initiatives and reduce their carbon footprint.

The Results
